Description about Amazon Website

Amazon is a global e-commerce platform that allows users to purchase a vast range of products online, including electronics, clothing, books, home essentials, groceries, and more. It offers features such as personalized recommendations, customer reviews, secure payment options, fast delivery (including same-day and next-day shipping through Amazon Prime), and order tracking.
